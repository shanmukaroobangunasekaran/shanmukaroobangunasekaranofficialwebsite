Bio-Statement

SHANMUKA ROOBAN GUNASEKARAN (Student Member, IEEE) received the B.Eng. degree (Hons.) in Communication Engineering in 2020 and the M.Sc. degree in Communication Engineering in 2020 and the M.Sc. degree in Communication Engineering in 2024, both from Universiti Malaysia Perlis (UniMAP), where he is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering. His research interests include reconfigurable antennas, metamaterials, dielectric resonator antennas, microwave sensing for agricultural applications, and artificial intelligence for quality detection. He has authored and coauthored several international conference and journal publications in these areas. Mr. Shanmuka has served as Chair of the IEEE UniMAP Student Branch and actively engages in professional and student activities. He is the recipient of multiple honors, including the IEEE Malaysia AP/MTT/EMC Joint Chapter Outstanding Master Thesis Award (2024), the IEEE Malaysia Student Volunteer Award (2024), and the Gold Award for Best Innovation at the Digital Leap Innovation Pitching 2025, held in conjunction with the 5G & OT Security Summit (5GOT) and the Ministry of Digital Malaysia, recognizing his contributions to secure and reconfigurable antenna technologies..
